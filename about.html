Our Founder

Joachim Ruzwe: Master Craftsman

Born and raised in KZN, Joachim brings over 15 years of woodworking expertise to every project. Trained under master European craftsmen before returning to Pietermaritzburg, he founded FastMove Carpenters to solve a local gap: premium carpentry without the months-long wait times.

"Wood doesn't lie. Either the joint fits or it doesn't. We believe in building carpentry solutions that stand as honestly as the timber itself."

Today, Joachim personally oversees every project from initial measurements to final finishes, ensuring FastMove's high standards are always met.
